Personal Information We Collect

Personal Information is information that can be used to identify an individual. We collect Personal Information any time the Services are used, both directly from individuals and through merchants or financial institutions. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Consumer Name
  • Contact information (email address, phone number, mailing address)
  • Order and payment information

We also collect:

  • IP address and device information
  • Merchant and merchant account information
  • Information from social media or website contact

Additionally, we collect aggregated or de-identified information for lawful purposes, which does not identify individuals.

Use of Cookies

Optimum Payments and its third-party service providers use cookies and similar tools to collect browsing information. These cookies improve performance, customize viewing, compile statistical reports, reduce fraud and enhance security.

Cookies also help recognize returning customers and assist with pre-populating forms. Such information may be disclosed to third-party providers acting on behalf of Optimum Payments.

Learn more about cookies and how to manage them at allaboutcookies.org.

We may also use “Flash cookies” for security and fraud detection. They are not managed by browser settings; visit Adobe’s Flash Player Help website for management instructions.
